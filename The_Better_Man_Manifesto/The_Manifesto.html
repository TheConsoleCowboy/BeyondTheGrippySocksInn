The Better Man Manifesto

What we practice manifests. What is it you want to manifest?

Work towards your strengths, share what you have to offer
In service with no ask for compensation.
Do not make life a grueling competition;
Instead, collaborate towards something better,
And leave the park much cleaner than when we found it.

Face conflict with your mind, not with your fists,
But know how to protect those faced with harm,
And how to take the world's blunt, bruising strikes,
On their behalf or yours, and keep your footing.
For no plan survives contact with reality.

Hold fast to your beliefs, though they may change,
For they are, in the end, your cornerstone;
But in your understanding of yourself,
Do not condemn or damn the views of others:

For we should love through learning others' minds,
And, forging good, seek first to understand.
